4‑Point Fat‑Loss Playbook
Carbs ≤ 15 % kcal (≤ 50 g net)

1. Eat to Burn, Not Store

  • Macros: 45 % protein · 40 % fat · ≤ 15 % carbs.
  • Meals: 3 fixed; add 1 protein snack only after night duty.
  • Proteins & Fats: eggs, sardines 3×/wk, salmon, chicken thighs, lamb, liver 1×/wk, kefir/cheese.
  • Allowed carbs (small): berries, avocado, leafy greens, lentils. No bread / rice / soda.
  • Daily supps: 2 g EPA‑DHA, vit D+K2, turmeric+pepper, magnesium.

2. Daily Movement Templates

3. Recovery & Hunger Control

  • Sleep 7 h; <6 h → skip HIIT & double fish oil.
  • Post‑workout: whey + tart cherry + collagen.
  • Night‑shift cravings: pack eggs + cheese; ignore vending carbs.
  • Tendon prep: eccentric calf & knee band work 2 × 15, 3×/wk.

4. Red Flags = Plateau

  • Cut >500 kcal (UCP2 thrifty) → stall.
  • HIIT two days straight → inflammation.
  • Skip meals after call → binge risk.
  • “Healthy” oat & fruit smoothies → insulin surge.
  • No movement 24 h → appetite > burn.
